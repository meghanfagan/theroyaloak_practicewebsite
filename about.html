Traditional English Pub Dining

Established in 1854, The Royal Oak has always been known as a place where anyone can take a seat, relax, and find comfort in rustic, traditional furnishings, and homemade and savory dishes. Our unique pairings, such as our signature fried macaroni with sausage, leeks, and breadcrumbs, are world-famous and guaranteed to satisfy any craving!

Along with a variety of specialty beers and ciders on tap, The Royal Oak also offers a variety of soft drinks and cream sodas for its younger patrons. Whether you are enjoying a pint after work, lunch with your family, or a night out with friends, The Royal Oak is the perfect place to leave your worries at the door and simply enjoy yourself.
